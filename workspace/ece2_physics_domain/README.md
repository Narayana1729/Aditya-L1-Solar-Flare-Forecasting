# ECE 2 — Physics & Validation Sandbox

Welcome to your independent workspace! You can write and test your physics-guided precursor scoring formulas and space weather validation logic here.

## Focus Areas
*   Formulating physics indices from Soft X-ray (SoLEXS) and Hard X-ray (HEL1OS) bands.
*   Pre-flare Brightening: Rate of Soft X-ray flux change.
*   Spectral Hardening: Energy ratio of high-energy vs. low-energy counts.
*   Evaluating space weather metrics: True Skill Statistic (TSS) and Heidke Skill Score (HSS).
